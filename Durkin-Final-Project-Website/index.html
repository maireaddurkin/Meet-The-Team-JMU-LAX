About Us

We are the 2021 James Madison Dukes Women's Lacrosse Team!

History

The JMU Lacrosse started in 1969 and became a part of the CAA in 1992. In 2018, JMU lacrosse won the national Championship, the first mid-major team to win the national title in women’s lacrosse history!

We are the reigning CAA regular season and conference champions from 2019 and recently won the programs 500th win after celebrating the 50th year of being a collegiate NCAA women’s lacrosse team.The current squad is pursuing its fourth consecutive CAA title this May.
